What is the Screen Test and How Does it Work?

Warhol Inspired

For anyone who has been fortunate enough to experience the screen test at the Warhol Museum...SORRY THIS ISNT THAT!

However, for those of you who have yet to experience it, the screen test is where you can let your "freak flag fly" or be as awkward and uncomforable as you want for ONE WHOLE MINUTE!

The videos will be recorded and the playback slowed down for some very awesome "Old Hollywood" effects! So sit back, do your thing, and have fun on the silve screen!
